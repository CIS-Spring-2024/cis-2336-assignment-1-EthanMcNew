Welcome!

Ethan McNew

Hello! I am the proud owner of Crazy McNew's Cafeteria. I am very excited to introduce this restaurant to the city of Sugar Land.

Born in this city, I grew up surrounded by culinary delights. I graduated from Stephen F. Austin High School, and decided to attend the University of Houston in 2022.

I've been to hundreds of restaurants in my life. Some of my favorites are Japanese, Chinese, buffets, and bars with lots of tasty food! When I had the idea of opening the cafeteria, I wanted to take the elements of my favorite restauraunts and put them all into my cafeteria.

In my spare time, I like to watch movies, play games, and draw. Onward towards the main cafeteria!
